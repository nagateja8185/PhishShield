About PhishShield

AI-powered cybersecurity for everyone

Our Mission

PhishShield was created with a simple mission: make phishing detection accessible to everyone. In an era where cyber threats are increasingly sophisticated, we believe that powerful security tools should not be limited to enterprises or technical experts.

By combining machine learning with an intuitive interface, PhishShield empowers users to identify and avoid phishing attacks before they cause harm.

Disclaimer

PhishShield provides automated analysis based on machine learning models trained on known phishing patterns. While we strive for high accuracy, no detection system is perfect.

Important:

  • This tool should supplement, not replace, human judgment
  • Always verify suspicious communications through official channels
  • When in doubt, contact the organization directly using known contact information
  • We are not responsible for any damages resulting from phishing attacks

Dataset Information

Our models are trained on publicly available datasets:

  • Email Dataset: 175,000+ labeled email samples (Safe and Phishing)
  • URL Dataset: 650,000+ labeled URLs (Benign, Phishing, Defacement)

These datasets enable our models to learn patterns associated with malicious content while maintaining high accuracy on legitimate communications.
